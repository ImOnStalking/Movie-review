The Misfits (2021)

Action Crime R

Directed by Renny Harlin | Starring Pierce Brosnan, Tim Roth, Nick Cannon, Mike Tyson

Runtime: 108 minutes
Release Date: June 11, 2021
Box Office: N/A (Direct-to-VOD)

Our Rating: 6/10

Movie Review

The Misfits is an action-comedy crime film directed by Renny Harlin, starring Pierce Brosnan as a con man who assembles a team of misfits for a heist. The film aims for humor and thrills but falls short in execution.

The plot follows Richard Pace, a smooth-talking con artist, who recruits a group of eccentric characters to pull off a daring robbery. The ensemble includes a boxer, a hacker, and a demolition expert, leading to comedic and action-packed sequences.

Strengths

  • Star Power: Pierce Brosnan brings charm to the lead role, and the supporting cast adds some entertainment value.
  • Action Sequences: Some of the heist and fight scenes are decently choreographed.
  • Concept: The idea of a ragtag team of misfits is fun and has potential.

Weaknesses

  • Poor Script: The dialogue is clunky, and the humor often falls flat.
  • Pacing Issues: The film drags in places and lacks momentum.
  • Underdeveloped Characters: The misfits feel stereotypical and one-dimensional.
  • Low Production Values: As a direct-to-VOD film, it shows in the budget constraints.

Final Thoughts

The Misfits tries to be a fun heist comedy but doesn't deliver on its promise. It's watchable for Brosnan fans but ultimately forgettable. Better heist films like Ocean's Eleven set a higher bar.

Overall, this is a 6/10. Entertaining in spots but not recommended.

Cast Highlights

  • Pierce Brosnan as Richard Pace
  • Tim Roth as Schultz
  • Nick Cannon as The Kid
  • Mike Tyson as Duke
