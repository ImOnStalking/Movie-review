The Choice (2016)

Romance Drama PG-13

Directed by Ross Katz | Starring Benjamin Walker, Teresa Palmer, Maggie Grace, Alexandra Vino, Tom Welling

Runtime: 111 minutes
Release Date: February 5, 2016
Box Office: $18.7 million worldwide

Our Rating: 6/10

Movie Review

The Choice is a romantic drama that explores love, loss, and difficult decisions. Based on Nicholas Sparks' novel, it follows Travis and Gabby as they navigate their relationship through joy and heartbreak.

The story centers on a young couple who meet and fall in love, but face a life-altering choice that tests their commitment and changes their lives forever.

Strengths

  • Emotional Depth: The film effectively captures the emotional turmoil of the characters' decisions.
  • Performances: Benjamin Walker and Teresa Palmer deliver heartfelt performances.
  • Visuals: Beautiful coastal settings enhance the romantic atmosphere.
  • Message: Thought-provoking exploration of love and sacrifice.

Weaknesses

  • Predictability: The plot follows familiar romantic drama tropes.
  • Pacing: Some scenes feel drawn out, slowing the emotional impact.
  • Character Development: Supporting characters lack depth.

Final Thoughts

The Choice is a poignant romantic drama that will appeal to fans of Nicholas Sparks' work. It's emotionally engaging but doesn't break new ground in the genre.

Overall, this is a 6/10. Solid performances, but predictable.

Cast Highlights

  • Benjamin Walker as Travis Parker
  • Teresa Palmer as Gabby Holland
  • Maggie Grace as Ryan Holland
  • Tom Welling as Ryan's Husband
