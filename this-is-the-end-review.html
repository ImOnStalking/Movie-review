This is the End (2013)

Comedy Horror Fantasy R

Directed by Seth Rogen, Evan Goldberg | Starring Seth Rogen, Jay Baruchel, James Franco, Jonah Hill, Danny McBride, Craig Robinson, Michael Cera, Emma Watson

Runtime: 107 minutes
Release Date: June 12, 2013
Box Office: $126 million worldwide

Our Rating: 6.6/10

Movie Review

This is the End is a hilarious mockumentary-style comedy where Seth Rogen and his friends, a group of Hollywood celebrities, find themselves trapped in James Franco's mansion during the apocalypse. As the world ends, they must confront their egos, fears, and past mistakes.

The film satirizes celebrity culture, friendship, and the end of the world, blending crude humor with unexpected depth. It's a meta-comedy that pokes fun at the actors playing themselves.

Strengths

  • Humor: Raunchy and irreverent, with plenty of laughs from the ensemble cast.
  • Cast Chemistry: The real-life friendships shine through, making the interactions authentic.
  • Meta Elements: Clever self-referential humor about Hollywood.
  • Visual Effects: Impressive apocalypse scenes.

Weaknesses

  • Pacing: Some parts feel slow or repetitive.
  • Raunchiness: May be too crude for some viewers.

Final Thoughts

This is the End is a fun, over-the-top comedy that's perfect for fans of Seth Rogen's style. It's not deep, but it's entertaining and surprisingly poignant at times.

Cast Highlights

  • Seth Rogen as Seth Rogen
  • James Franco as James Franco
  • Jay Baruchel as Jay Baruchel
  • Jonah Hill as Jonah Hill
